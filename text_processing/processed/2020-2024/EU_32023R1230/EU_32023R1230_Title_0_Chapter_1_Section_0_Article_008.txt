Article 8
Essential health and safety requirements for products within the scope of this Regulation
Machinery or related products shall only be made available on the market or put into service if, where properly installed and maintained and used for their intended use or under conditions which can reasonably be foreseen, they meet the essential health and safety requirements set out in AnnexÂ III.
Partly completed machinery shall only be made available on the market if it meets the relevant essential health and safety requirements set out in Annex III.
