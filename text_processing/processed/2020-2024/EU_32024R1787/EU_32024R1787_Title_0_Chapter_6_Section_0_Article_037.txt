Article 37
Amendment to Regulation (EU) 2019/942
In Article 15 of Regulation (EU) 2019/942 the following paragraph is added:
‘8.   Every 3 years, ACER, after consulting Member States, shall establish and make publicly available a set of indicators and corresponding reference values for the comparison of unit investment costs linked to measurement, quantification, monitoring, reporting, verification and reduction, including venting and flaring, of methane emissions for comparable projects. It shall issue recommendations on indicators and reference values for unit investment costs for the purposes of complying with the obligations under Regulation (EU) 2024/1787 of the European Parliament and of the Council (*1) as provided in Article 3 of that Regulation.
