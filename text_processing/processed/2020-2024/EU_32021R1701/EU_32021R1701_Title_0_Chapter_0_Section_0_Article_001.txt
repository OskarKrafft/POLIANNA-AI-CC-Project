Article 1
Regulation (EU) 2020/2222 is amended as follows:
(1)
Article 3 is amended as follows:
(a)
in paragraph 2, the first sentence is replaced by the following:
‘2.   The safety certificates referred to in point (b) of Article 1(2) shall remain valid for 15 months from the date of application of this Regulation.’;
(b)
in paragraph 3, the first sentence is replaced by the following:
‘3.   The licences referred to in point (c) of Article 1(2) shall remain valid for 15 months from the date of application of this Regulation.’;
(2)
in Article 8, paragraph 3 is replaced by the following:
‘3.   This Regulation shall cease to apply on 31 March 2022.’.
