Article 269
Specific provisions regarding the EEAS
The EEAS shall be subject to the procedures provided for in Article 319 TFEU and in Articles 266, 267 and 268 of this Regulation. The EEAS shall fully cooperate with Union institutions involved in the discharge procedure and provide, as appropriate, any additional necessary information, including through attendance at meetings of the relevant bodies.
