Article 160
Indirect management with Member State organisations
1.   The Commission may, in accordance with Article 62(1), first subparagraph, points (c)(v) and (vi), implement the budget indirectly with Member State organisations.
2.   Where the Commission implements the budget indirectly with Member States organisations, it shall rely on the systems, rules and procedures of those organisations which have been assessed in accordance with Article 157(2), (3) and (4).
3.   Financial framework partnership agreements concluded with Member State organisations in accordance with Article 131 shall further specify the extent and the modalities of the cross-reliance on systems, rules and procedures of Member State organisations and may include specific provisions on the cross-reliance on assessments and audits as referred to in Articles 126 and 127.
