Article 161
Indirect management with third countries
1.   The Commission may implement the budget indirectly with a third country or the bodies designated by that country, as referred to in Article 62(1), first subparagraph, point (c)(i), by concluding a financing agreement describing the Union’s intervention in the third country and laying down the method of implementation for each part of the action.
2.   For the part of the action implemented indirectly with the third country or the bodies it has designated, the financing agreement shall, in addition to the elements referred to in Article 158(7), clearly define the roles and responsibilities of the third country and of the Commission in the implementation of the funds. The financing agreement shall also determine the rules and procedures to be applied by the third country when implementing Union funds.
