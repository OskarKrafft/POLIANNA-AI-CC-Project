Article 36
Amendment to Directive (EU) 2019/1937
In Part I.C.1 of the Annex to Directive (EU) 2019/1937, the following point is added:
‘(iv)
Regulation (EU) 2024/3015 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 November 2024 on prohibiting products made with forced labour on the Union market and amending Directive (EU) 2019/1937 (OJ L, 2024/3015, 12.12.2024, ELI: http://data.europa.eu/eli/reg/2024/3015/oj).’.
