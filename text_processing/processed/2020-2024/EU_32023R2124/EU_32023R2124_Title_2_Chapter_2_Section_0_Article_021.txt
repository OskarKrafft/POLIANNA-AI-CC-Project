Article 21
Logbook
Notwithstanding Article 14(1) of Regulation (EC) No 1224/2009, authorised fishing vessels operating within the scope of this section shall have on board a logbook in which daily catches of giant red shrimp and blue and red shrimp are recorded and declared, irrespective of the live weight of the catch, in accordance with Article 18 of this Regulation.
