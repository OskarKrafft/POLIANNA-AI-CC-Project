Article 35
Fishing activities
Member States shall communicate to the Commission by 15 November of each year, a detailed report on fishing activities of fishing vessels operating within the scope of this section for the previous year. The Commission shall transmit that report to the GFCM Secretariat by 30 November of each year. That report shall include at least the following:
(1)
operating days;
(2)
operating area;
(3)
total catch of key species listed in Article 31.
