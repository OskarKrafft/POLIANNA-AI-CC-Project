Article 133
Access to information and data related to monitoring, control and surveillance within the framework of joint inspection and surveillance schemes
1.   This Article shall apply to Member States engaged in pilot projects or international schemes for joint inspection and surveillance in the GFCM area of application.
2.   Member States shall ensure the secure treatment of the information, data, reports and messages related to monitoring, control and surveillance within the framework of joint inspection and surveillance schemes.
3.   This Article shall be without prejudice to the obligations of Member States relating to their processing of personal data under Union law.
