Article 37
Designated landing points
1.   Member States shall designate landing points where the landings by vessels actively fishing within the scope of this section shall take place.
2.   It shall be prohibited to land or tranship from fishing vessels any quantity of species listed in Article 31 at any place other than landing points designated by Member States in accordance with paragraph 1.
3.   Fishers or masters of authorised fishing vessels actively fishing within the scope of this section shall declare all catches of the species listed in Article 31, irrespective of the live weight of the catch.
