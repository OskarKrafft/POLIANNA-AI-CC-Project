Article 19
Designated landing points
1.   Member States shall designate landing points where landings and transhipping by vessels actively fishing within the scope of this section shall take place. For each designated landing point, Member States shall specify permitted landing and transhipping times and places.
2.   It shall be prohibited to land or tranship from fishing vessels any quantity of species included within the scope of this section at any place other than landing points designated by Member States in accordance with paragraph 1.
3.   Member States shall communicate to the Commission any change to the list of designated landing points by 15 November of each year. The Commission shall transmit that list to the GFCM Secretariat by 30 November of each year.
