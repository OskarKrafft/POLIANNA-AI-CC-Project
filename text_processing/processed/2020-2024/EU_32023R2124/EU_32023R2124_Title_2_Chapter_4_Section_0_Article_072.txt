Article 72
Reporting of catches
1.   Authorised trawl vessels, fishing within the scope of this section, shall be equipped with an appropriate system of geopositioning. Authorised trawl vessels above 12 metres in LOA shall be equipped with a VMS. For authorised trawl vessels below 12 metres in LOA, Member States shall communicate to the Commission the chosen system of geopositioning.
2.   All catches of key stocks listed in Article 66, irrespectively of the live weight of the catch, as well as catches of non-target species in excess of 50 kg, shall be reported in the logbook.
