Article 75
Control, monitoring and surveillance of fisheries for small pelagic stocks in the Adriatic Sea
1.   By 1 October of each year, Member States shall communicate to the Commission their plans and programmes to ensure compliance with Article 74 through adequate monitoring and reporting, in particular of the monthly catches and fishing effort deployed.
2.   The Commission shall submit the information referred to in paragraph 1 to the GFCM Secretariat by 30 October of each year.
