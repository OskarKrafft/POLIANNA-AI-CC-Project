Article 102
Incidental catches of seabirds in fishing gear
1.   Masters of fishing vessels shall promptly release seabirds incidentally caught in fishing gear.
2.   Fishing vessels shall not bring seabirds ashore except within the framework of national plans for the conservation of seabirds or to secure assistance for the recovery of harmed individual seabirds, and provided that the competent national authorities have been duly and officially informed, prior to the fishing vessel concerned returning to port, of the intention to bring such seabirds ashore.
