ArticleÂ 25
Fishing activities
Member States shall communicate to the Commission by 31 July of each year, a detailed report on fishing activities of fishing vessels operating within the scope of this section for the previous year. The Commission shall transmit that report to the GFCM Secretariat by 31 August of each year. That report shall include at least the following:
(1)
operating days;
(2)
operating area;
(3)
total catch.
