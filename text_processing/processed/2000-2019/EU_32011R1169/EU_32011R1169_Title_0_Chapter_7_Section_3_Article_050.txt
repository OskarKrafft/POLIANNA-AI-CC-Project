Article 50
Amendments to Regulation (EC) No 1925/2006
Paragraph 3 of Article 7 of Regulation (EC) No 1925/2006 is replaced by the following:
‘3.   Nutrition labelling of products to which vitamins and minerals have been added and which are covered by this Regulation shall be compulsory. The information to be provided shall consist of that specified in Article 30(1) of Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 25 October 2011 on the provision of food information to consumers (*2) and of the total amounts present of the vitamins and minerals when added to the food.
