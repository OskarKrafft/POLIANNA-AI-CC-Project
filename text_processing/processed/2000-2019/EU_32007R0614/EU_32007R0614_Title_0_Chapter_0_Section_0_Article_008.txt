Article 8
Participation of third countries
Programmes financed through LIFE+ shall be open to the participation of the following countries, provided that supplementary appropriations are received:
(a)
EFTA States which have become members of the European Environment Agency in accordance with Council Regulation (EC) No 933/1999 of 29 April 1999 amending Regulation (EEC) No 1210/90 on the establishment of the European Environment Agency and the European environment information and observation network (11);
(b)
candidate countries for accession to the European Union;
(c)
Western Balkan countries included in the Stabilisation and Association Process.
