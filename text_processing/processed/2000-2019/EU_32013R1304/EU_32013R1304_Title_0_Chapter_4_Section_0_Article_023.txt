Article 23
Financial management
In addition to Article 130 of Regulation (EU) NoÂ 1303/2013, when the Commission reimburses interim payments and pays the final balance for the YEI by priority axis, it shall allocate the reimbursement from the budget of the Union equally between the ESF and the specific allocation for YEI. Once all resources from the specific allocation for YEI have been reimbursed, the Commission shall allocate the remaining reimbursements from the budget of the Union to the ESF.
The Commission shall allocate the reimbursement from the ESF between categories of regions according to the ratio laid down in Article 22 (2).
