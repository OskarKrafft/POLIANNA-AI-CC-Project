Article 103
Structure of the standard formula
The Solvency Capital Requirement calculated on the basis of the standard formula shall be the sum of the following items:
(a)
the Basic Solvency Capital Requirement, as laid down in Article 104;
(b)
the capital requirement for operational risk, as laid down in Article 107;
(c)
the adjustment for the loss-absorbing capacity of technical provisions and deferred taxes, as laid down in Article 108.
