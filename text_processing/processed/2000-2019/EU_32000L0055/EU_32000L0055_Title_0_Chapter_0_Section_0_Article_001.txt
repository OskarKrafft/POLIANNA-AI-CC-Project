Article 1
1. This Directive shall apply to electric mains-operated ballasts for fluorescent lighting sources as defined in European Standard EN 50294 of December 1998, point 3.4, and referred to hereinafter as "ballasts".
2. The following types of ballasts are excluded from this Directive:
- ballasts integrated in lamps,
- ballasts designed specifically for luminaires to be mounted in furniture and which form a non-replaceable part of the luminaire which cannot be tested separately from the luminaire (according to European Standard EN 60920, clause 2.1.3), and
- ballasts to be exported from the Community, either as a single component or incorporated in luminaires.
3. Ballasts shall be classified in accordance with Annex I.
