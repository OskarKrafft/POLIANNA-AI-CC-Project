Article 5
Bilateral agreements or arrangements
The Member States shall neither negotiate nor enter into any bilateral agreements or arrangements with the United Kingdom on matters falling within the scope of this Regulation with respect to the period during which this Regulation applies.Â With respect to that period, they shall not otherwise grant United Kingdom road haulage operators or United Kingdom coach and bus service operators any rights other than those granted in this Regulation, without prejudice to existing multilateral arrangements.
