Article 5
Amendments to Regulation (EU) No 652/2014
Regulation (EU) No 652/2014 is amended as follows:
(1)
in Article 4, the following paragraph is added:
‘4.   In the case of approval of multiannual actions, budgetary commitments may be divided into annual instalments. Where budgetary commitments are so divided, the Commission shall commit the annual instalments taking into account the progress of the actions, the estimated needs and the budget available.’;
(2)
in Article 13, paragraph 5 is deleted;
(3)
in Article 22, paragraph 5 is deleted;
(4)
in Article 27, paragraph 5 is deleted.
