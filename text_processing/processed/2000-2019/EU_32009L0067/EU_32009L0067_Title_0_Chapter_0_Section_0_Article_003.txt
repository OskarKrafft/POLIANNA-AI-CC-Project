Article 3
1.   In accordance with Article 11 of Directive 2002/24/EC, equivalence between the requirements laid down in this Directive for two-wheel motorcycles and those laid down in UNECE Regulation No 53 is hereby acknowledged.
2.   The authorities of the Member States which grant EC component type-approval shall accept approvals granted in accordance with the requirements of UNECE Regulation No 53 as well as component type-approval marks as an alternative to the corresponding approvals granted in accordance with the requirements of this Directive.
