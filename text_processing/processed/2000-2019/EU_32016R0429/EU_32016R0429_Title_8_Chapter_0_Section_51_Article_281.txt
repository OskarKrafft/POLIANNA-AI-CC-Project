Article 281
Relation with acts concerning official controls
In the event of any conflict between the provisions of this Regulation and the provisions of Regulation (EC) No 882/2004, Council Directives 89/608/EEC (66), 89/662/EEC, 90/425/EEC, 91/496/EEC, 96/93/EC (67) and 97/78/EC and Decision 92/438/EEC, the provisions of this Regulation shall prevail.
