Article 44
Coordination of notified bodies
The Commission shall ensure appropriate coordination and cooperation between bodies notified under this Directive through the establishment of a sectoral group of notified bodies. The Agency shall support the activities of notified bodies in accordance with Article 24 of Regulation (EU)Â 2016/796.
Member States shall ensure that the bodies notified by them participate in the work of that group, directly or by means of designated representatives.
