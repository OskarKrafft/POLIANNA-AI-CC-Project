Article 48
Amendments to Directive 2009/31/EC
Directive 2009/31/EC is amended as follows:
(1)
in Article 27, paragraph 1 is replaced by the following:
‘Every four years the Member States shall submit to the Commission a report on the implementation of this Directive, including the register referred to in point (b) of Article 25(1). The first report shall be sent to the Commission by 30 June 2011. The report shall be drawn up on the basis of a questionnaire or outline adopted by the Commission in the form of implementing acts. Those implementing acts shall be adopted in accordance with the examination procedure referred to in Article 30(2). The questionnaire or outline shall be sent to Member States at least six months before the deadline for the submission of the report.’;
(2)
in Article 38, paragraph 1 is deleted.
