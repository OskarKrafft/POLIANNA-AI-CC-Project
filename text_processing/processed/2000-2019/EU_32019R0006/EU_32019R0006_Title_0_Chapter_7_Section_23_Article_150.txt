Article 150
Relation with other Union acts
1.   Nothing in this Regulation shall be understood to affect the provisions of Directive 96/22/EC.
2.   Commission Regulation (EC) No 1234/2008 (30) shall not apply to veterinary medicinal products covered by this Regulation.
3.   Commission Regulation (EC) No 658/2007 (31) shall not apply to veterinary medicinal products covered by this Regulation.
