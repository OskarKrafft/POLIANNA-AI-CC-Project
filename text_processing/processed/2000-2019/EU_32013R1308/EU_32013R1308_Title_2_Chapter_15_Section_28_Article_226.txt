Article 226
Use of the Reserve
Funds transferred from the Reserve for crises in the agricultural sector under the conditions and procedure referred to in Article 25 of Regulation (EU) NoÂ 1306/2013 and paragraph 22 of the Interinstitutional Agreement between the European Parliament, the Council and the Commission on budgetary discipline, cooperation in budgetary matters and on sound financial management shall be made available for the measures to which this Regulation applies for the year or years for which the additional support is required and which are implemented in circumstances that go beyond normal market developments.
In particular, funds shall be transferred for any expenditure under:
(a)
Articles 8 to 21;
(b)
Articles 196 to 204; and
(c)
Articles 219, 220 and 221 of this Regulation.
