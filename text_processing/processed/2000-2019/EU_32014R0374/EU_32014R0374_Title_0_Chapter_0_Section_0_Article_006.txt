Article 6
Committee procedure
1.   For the implementation of Article 3(2) and Article 4 of this Regulation, the Commission shall be assisted by the Customs Code Committee established by Article 248a of Council Regulation (EEC) No 2913/92 (5). That committee shall be a committee within the meaning of Regulation (EU) No 182/2011.
2.   Where reference is made to this paragraph, Article 5 of Regulation (EU) No 182/2011 shall apply.
